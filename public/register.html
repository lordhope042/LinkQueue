Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use LinkQueue:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password (encrypted)
  • Usage Data: Queue positions, session times, and feature usage
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security
  • Google Profile Data: When using Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email, and profile picture (only with your consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our queue management services
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To send important notifications about your queues
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including bcrypt hashing, HTTPS/TLS encryption, JWT tokens with automatic expiration, and rate limiting.

4. Google Sign-In Data

When you use Google Sign-In, we only request access to your basic profile (name, email, picture). Your Google credentials are never stored on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your account and data
  • Right to export your data

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: privacy@linkqueue.com
